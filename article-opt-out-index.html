This page is designed to be updated over time. Add new sites, platforms, retailers, app settings, ad preferences, and privacy exits here as you find them. Keep each entry short, direct, and useful.

How to use this page

  • List the company or platform name.
  • Link directly to its opt-out or privacy controls when possible.
  • Note any friction, account requirements, or misleading wording.
  • Update regularly as settings move or policies change.
