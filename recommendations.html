Read before accepting

Look for arbitration clauses, data-sharing language, retention windows, and opt-out mechanics before treating a checkbox as routine.

Reduce the surface

Disable tracking toggles where possible, avoid unnecessary accounts, and keep permissions scoped to the task at hand.

Prefer portable exits

Favor services that let you export, delete, or move your data without turning refusal into a support ticket.
