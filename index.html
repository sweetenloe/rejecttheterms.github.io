Why this exists

Most people click through. The system depends on that.

Terms and Conditions are often treated as ceremony: a screen to dismiss, a box to check, a ritual that moves you toward access. But those pages decide more than access. They define what gets collected, what gets retained, what gets inferred, and what gets normalized.
